Our fiber laser system handles virtually any surface contaminant without abrasion, without chemicals, and without damage to the base material underneath.

PART NO. SVC-001 // METAL
Rust Removal

Vaporize iron oxide corrosion down to bare, clean metal with surgical precision. Frames, equipment, tools, axles, and structural components of any size.

PART NO. SVC-002 // MULTI-SURFACE
Paint Stripping

Strip layers of paint, powder coat, epoxy, or varnish with zero damage to the substrate. Works on metal, concrete, and wood surfaces.

PART NO. SVC-003 // WOOD & STONE
Wood Restoration

Remove stains, charring, graffiti, and biological growth from wood while preserving the grain and structural integrity underneath.

PART NO. SVC-004 // INDUSTRIAL

PART NO. SVC-005 // WELD & FAB

Oxide-free surface preparation for welding or coating. Remove heat discoloration and weld spatter in minutes, not hours.
