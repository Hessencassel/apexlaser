The Difference

Why Laser Cleaning
Changes Everything

Traditional rust and paint removal is messy, damaging, and expensive. Laser cleaning is the modern alternative that delivers better results with less labor and zero consumables.

The Old Way
  • ✕ Sandblasting creates massive mess and media waste
  • ✕ Chemical stripping involves toxic, hazardous substances
  • ✕ Grinding risks gouging and damaging the base material
  • ✕ Abrasives alter surface dimensions and part tolerances
  • ✕ Media cleanup adds hours and cost to every job
  • ✕ Chemicals require special handling, PPE, and disposal fees
  • ✕ Inconsistent results on complex geometries and contours
Laser Cleaning
  • ✓ Non-contact — the laser never physically touches the surface
  • ✓ Zero chemicals, zero media, zero hazardous waste
  • ✓ Selectively removes contaminants, leaves base material intact
  • ✓ Maintains tight tolerances on precision components
  • ✓ Minimal cleanup — contaminants are vaporized or vacuumed
  • ✓ Safe, eco-friendly, EPA-compliant process
  • ✓ Precise control on any geometry, weld, or contour
